Thank you very much for installing Satisfying Clicks!

The satisfying click effects will work on all* new pages you visit from now on. You can always customize or disable the effects by clicking on the extension in your toolbar. *due to Google restrictions, the extension can't work on browser-specific pages like the settings page or your startup page.

Below you'll find some Frequently Asked Questions (and answers!). If you have more questions or feedback, you can mail me here.

How do I use Satisfying Clicks?
Satisfying Clicks works on all open pages automatically. Just visit any website and click anywhere. You'll see fun click effects that you can customize by clicking the extension in your toolbar.
Can you implement my idea for a new Click-Effect design?
Sure! Feel free to drop a message with your idea and design. If it fits and works from a technical perspective, we can make it happen!
What's the rare click animation?
The rare animation has a 1 in 1,000 chance of appearing when you click. It's a small but exciting surprise! Keep on clicking and see if you can find it.
Does this extension track my clicks or collect data?
